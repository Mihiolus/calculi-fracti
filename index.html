Calculi Fracti: A Roman fractional calculator

Style for large numbers
Display of Arabic fractions

N.B.: Only valid Roman numbers are displayed, i.e. greater or equal to 112 or 1 uncia, and less than 500,000 for Roman numerals and 10,000,000 for word expressions.

Legend for fractions
  • S = 612 or 12
  • ⁙ = 512
  • ∷ = 412 or 13
  • ∴ = 312 or 14
  • : = 212 or 16
  • · = 112
  • Є = 124
  • Ͻ = 148
  • 𐆓 = 172
  • 𐆔 = 1144
  • Э = 1288
